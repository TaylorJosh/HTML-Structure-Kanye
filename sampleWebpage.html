Kanye's best song?

Kanye West is one of the most influential artists of my generation. Since his introduction as a producer, Kanye has changed the landscape of hip hop music. Below, I will make a case for Jesus Walks and Runaway as Kanye’s best songs.

Jesus Walks

This is arguably Kanye’s best song. Created in a sea of misogyny and violence, “Jesus Walks” was a shining light for a perpetually distasteful genre. Growing up as a hip hop fan and a devout Christian, it was hard to find a common ground. Kanye’s Jesus Walks not only filled that void, but also encouraged forced other popular hip hop artists to be more vulnerable about religious beliefs.

Runaway

My beautiful dark twisted fantasy is Kanye’s best album, and the best song on the album is “Runaway.” Runaway shows a different side of Kanye. He was vulnerable and apologetic. The production was beyond anything that we had ever heard from Kanye, and the accompanying video was theatrical. As with most of Kanye’s material, Runaway and MBDTF changed hip hop artists and consumers.
